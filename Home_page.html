FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is Money Mitra safe and regulated?
Yes. Money Mitra operates under RBI guidelines and is ISO 27001 certified. All deposits up to ₹5 Lakhs are insured under the DICGC scheme. We use 256-bit SSL encryption and AI-powered fraud detection to keep your funds safe.
How long does account opening take?
The registration process takes under 3 minutes. Submit your PAN and Aadhaar for KYC — our admin team verifies and issues your unique CKYC number. You get your account number, IFSC, and UPI ID instantly upon approval.
What are the charges for UPI transfers?
Absolutely zero. All UPI, IMPS, and internal transfers are completely free. We believe banking should not cost you money to move your own money. NEFT transfers are also fee-free on Money Mitra.
What interest rate do savings accounts earn?
Our savings accounts earn 3.5% p.a. with zero minimum balance requirements. Premium accounts with higher balances qualify for up to 7% p.a. through our Smart Savings feature with automated sweep deposits.
Can I close my account at any time?
Yes, you can close your account at any time from the Profile → Security → Danger Zone section. Account closure requires password verification. Admins can also close accounts if required. All remaining balance will be transferred before closure.
How do I contact support?
24/7 support via the Support tab in your dashboard. Raise a ticket, chat with our team, or browse the FAQ. Admin responses are typically within minutes for critical issues and a few hours for general queries.
